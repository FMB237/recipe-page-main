Simple Omelette recipe

An easy and quick dish,perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combine beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice cheese ,Vegetables or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approxiamely 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredient

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 table spoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling:cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs:In a bowl,beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed .You can add tablespoon of water or milk for fluttier texture
  2. Heat the pan:place a non-stick frying pan our medium heat and butter or oil
  3. Cook the omelette:Once the butter is melt and bubbling,pour in the eggs .Tilt the pan to ensure the eggs coat the surface.
  4. Add fillings(Optional):When the eggs begin to set at the edges but are still slightly running in the middle sprinkle your choosen fillings over one half of the omelette.
  5. Food and serve:As the omelette continue to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings.Let it cook for another minutes, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. Enjoy:Self hot with,additional salt and pepper if needed
